# Cookbook Contributors

Contributing a cookbook to HealthChain is a real, citable open-source contribution.

**Your name on the work.** Once your use case is merged, you're listed below with your name, affiliation, and a link to your contribution. Every versioned release is archived on Zenodo with a persistent DOI — citable in papers, CVs, and grant applications.

**A grant support letter if you need one.** A template is at [`docs/contributor-resources/grant-support-letter-template.md`](./docs/contributor-resources/grant-support-letter-template.md). Email jenniferjiangkells@gmail.com with subject `[Cookbook Contributor] Support Request`. Allow 5 working days.

**Direct maintainer support throughout.** From scoping to merge, a maintainer will help with architecture, review drafts, and answer integration questions.

To get started, comment on [issue #208](https://github.com/healthchainai/HealthChain/issues/208) with your proposed use case.
